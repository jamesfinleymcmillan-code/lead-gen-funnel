# Facebook Ads Strategy - $200 Budget

## Setup Steps (Do First)

### 1. Get Facebook Pixel ID
1. Go to https://business.facebook.com/events_manager
2. Create a pixel (or copy existing pixel ID)
3. Add to `.env.local`: `NEXT_PUBLIC_FB_PIXEL_ID=your_pixel_id_here`
4. Deploy the updated site

### 2. Create Facebook Business Manager Account
- Go to https://business.facebook.com
- Set up payment method
- Verify your business

## Campaign Strategy - $200 Budget Split

### Campaign 1: Website Traffic Campaign ($120/week)
**Objective:** Traffic
**Daily Budget:** $17/day
**Duration:** 7 days

**Targeting:**
- Location: Australia (or your target area)
- Age: 25-55
- Interests:
  - Small business
  - Entrepreneurship
  - Web design
  - Digital marketing
  - Business services

**Ad Creative:**
- Headline: "Professional Website in 7 Days - From $500"
- Text: "Get a custom website that brings you customers. Fixed pricing, fast delivery, no hidden fees. Book this week and save 10%."
- Image: Screenshot of your best work or the landing page hero
- CTA Button: "Learn More"

### Campaign 2: Conversion Campaign ($80/week)
**Objective:** Conversions (InitiateCheckout event)
**Daily Budget:** $11/day
**Duration:** 7 days

**Targeting:**
- Location: Australia
- Age: 30-60
- Interests:
  - Small business owners
  - Website builders
  - eCommerce
  - Local business

**Ad Creative:**
- Headline: "Limited Time: 10% Off Professional Websites"
- Text: "Only this week - save 10% on custom website development. Basic package starts at $500. Fast delivery, professional results."
- Image: Before/after website comparison or pricing table
- CTA Button: "Get Quote"

## Testing Strategy

**Week 1-2:** Test 3 different ad creatives
- Run all 3 ads with $20 each
- See which gets best CTR (click-through rate)
- Turn off worst performer
- Double budget on best performer

**Week 3-4:** Optimize winning ad
- Test different audiences
- Test different images
- Increase budget to $30/day if ROI is positive

## Success Metrics

**Track These:**
- Cost Per Click (CPC): Target under $0.50
- Click-Through Rate (CTR): Target above 2%
- Cost Per InitiateCheckout: Target under $20
- Cost Per Purchase: Target under $200

**If after $100 spent:**
- No sales = pause, fix landing page or offer
- 1+ sales = keep going, increase budget

## Free Traffic Tactics (While Running Ads)

### 1. Local Business Groups
- Join Facebook groups for local businesses
- Answer questions, offer value
- Mention your service when relevant
- Don't spam - be genuinely helpful

### 2. LinkedIn Outreach (Free)
- Connect with 20 local business owners per day
- Send personalized message:
  "Hey [Name], I help [industry] businesses get more customers through professional websites. Would love to connect!"

### 3. Cold Email (Free)
- Find businesses with bad websites
- Email: "Hi [Name], I noticed [specific issue with their site]. I specialize in building websites for [industry]. Would you be open to a quick chat about improving your online presence?"

### 4. Partner with the Cold Caller
- Give him this landing page URL
- Track manually by asking customers "how did you hear about us?"
- Pay him 10-20% per sale

### 5. Reddit/Forums
- r/smallbusiness
- r/entrepreneur
- Answer questions about websites
- Link to your site in profile

## Quick Wins

1. **Retargeting Pixel**: Everyone who visits your site will be tracked. In 2 weeks, create a retargeting campaign showing ads to people who visited but didn't buy. These convert at 5-10x higher rates.

2. **Lookalike Audiences**: After 10 purchases, create a "lookalike audience" in Facebook - it will find people similar to your customers.

3. **Testimonials**: Get testimonials from first 3 customers, add to ads immediately.

## Budget Scaling

If you get 1 sale from $200:
- Break even or slightly profitable
- Keep running, optimize creative

If you get 2+ sales from $200:
- Scale to $300-500/week
- Profit pays for ads

If you get 0 sales from $200:
- Stop ads
- Fix landing page (test different headlines, pricing, or offer)
- Test again with $100
