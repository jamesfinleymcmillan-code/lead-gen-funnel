## What Happens Next?

### After Publishing:
- Your ad goes into review (expect 1-24 hours)
- Once approved, it starts running automatically
- Budget: Check your campaign settings (should be $17/day for Traffic campaign)

### Monitoring Your Ad:
- Go to Ads Manager daily to check performance
- Track these metrics:
  - **CTR (Click-Through Rate)**: Target above 2%
  - **CPC (Cost Per Click)**: Target under $0.50
  - **Link Clicks**: How many people visited your site
  - **Cost Per Result**: How much each website visitor costs
